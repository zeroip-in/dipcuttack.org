About Our University

At a Glance

With a humble beginning in 2005, Dhabaleswar Institute of Polytechnic (DIP) has successfully placed itself in the forefront of technical education, not only in the state but in the region as a whole. Backed by a robust infrastructure and a sprawling campus in over 1,50,000 sq ft, DIP is offering diploma courses in Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Engineering at its polytechnic, Fitter and Electrical trades at its own ITI and CNC Operation at the state-of-art skill development centre.

A team of over 150 professionals are out there shaping the young talents and transforming individuals to become allround techno professionals.
