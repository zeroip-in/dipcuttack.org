About DIP

DIP At a Glancey

With a humble beginning in 2005, Dhabaleswar Institute of Polytechnic (DIP) has successfully placed itself in the forefront of technical education, not only in the state but in the region as a whole. Backed by a robust infrastructure and a sprawling campus in over 1,50,000 sq ft, DIP is offering diploma courses in Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Engineering at its polytechnic, Fitter and Electrical trades at its own ITI and CNC Operation at the state-of-art skill development centre. A team of over 150 professionals are out there shaping the young talents and transforming individuals to become allround techno professionals.

Training & Placement

The placement cell of Institute provides guidance and all the assistance to the students for achieving their career goals. The cell takes relevant steps in identifying the demands of the industry and prepares the students towards this need.

Adequate emphasis is given for soft skills development along with the regular academic program. Group discussions and aptitude tests are conducted at regular intervals to enable the students to improve their performance during placements.
